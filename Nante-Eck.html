Nante Eck - a UX/UI Case Study

Nante Eck was a case study completed for my "Web Design" course in the Digital Women Program at ReDI School of Digital Integration. It is the website redesign for Nante Eck, a historical restaurant in central Berlin, that allows potential customers to reserve tables, explore food menus, find directions, and get additional information about the restaurant.
