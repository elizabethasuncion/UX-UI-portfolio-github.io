Perfect Properties - a UI Case Study

Perfect Properties was a case study completed for my "UI for UX Designers" specialization course. It is a responsive web app for people looking to buy, rent, and sell homes through an extensive database of residential properties and land. It allows users to explore comprehensive listing information and easily connect with agents, making property investment stress free and accessible for all types of investors.
